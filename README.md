# 🎈 Compliment Balloon

A cute interactive web app where you send someone a link, they tell you who you are to them, and they get to pop balloons filled with AI-generated compliments!

## What it does:

1. **You create a balloon link** and send it to someone (friend, crush, anyone!)

2. **They open the link** and answer: "Who am I to you?"
   - Crush 😳
   - Best Friend 💕
   - Friend ✨
   - Person I Know 👋
   - Other... 🤔 (they can write their own!)

3. **AI generates 6 custom compliments** based on their answer
   - If they pick "crush" → flirty compliments
   - If they pick "best friend" → loyal friendship vibes
   - If they write "my cat's favorite human" → compliments about that!

4. **They pop each balloon** to reveal the compliments
   - Each pop has a different effect: hearts 💕, stars ✨, bubbles 🫧, lightning ⚡, flowers 🌸, or paint 🎨
   - Satisfying animations make them want to pop all 6

5. **They can screenshot and share** - it's cute chaos!
